About Me

I'm SANGARE CHEICK CHAKA, a passionate soccer player from Abidjan, Côte d'ivoire, with a dream to combine academic excellence with athletic achievement at a top American university.

I began my soccer career at the age of 8 at the local Espoir de Koumassi academy, before continuing my training at the Centre de Formation Atlantique de Koumassi (CFAK). Step by step, I progressed until I joined the Academy Saint Emmanuel, one of the leading regional clubs in Côte d'Ivoire for the development of young talent. Thanks to my technical skills, my vision of the game, and my leadership qualities, I was able to distinguish myself and gain recognition in several regional tournaments.

Beyond the pitch, I remain committed to growing both as a player and as a person. My ambition is to compete at the highest possible level, whether through NCAA/NAIA programs or other international opportunities. I am open to every pathway that allows me to combine competitive soccer with personal development. I believe this balance will help me evolve into a well‑rounded individual, prepared to succeed both on and off the field.

Objective: To join a competitive soccer program at the national or international level where I can contribute my skills, continue to grow as a player, and embrace opportunities for both athletic and personal development.
